About Us


Founded in 2000 , CAD Services has built a strong reputation for delivering precise and dependable surveying solutions across a wide range of infrastructure projects. With nearly two decades of experience, we offer expertise in Bridge Surveys, Land and Road Surveys, as well as advanced surveying using Total Station and DGPS technology. Our scope also includes Layout Work and Center Line Marking, ensuring accuracy from planning to execution. We are also committed to capacity-building through structured Training Programs that equip aspiring professionals with practical field knowledge. CAD Services is built on a foundation of technical excellence, integrity, and long-standing client trust.


Our Services


Land Survey using DGPS and Total Station


CAD Services offers precise Land Surveying using advanced DGPS and Total Station technology. Our surveys ensure accurate boundary mapping, topographic data, and layout marking for construction and development projects. With high-precision instruments and skilled professionals, we deliver reliable results essential for planning, design, and execution across various terrains.

Bridge Survey


We deliver accurate and reliable bridge surveys essential for planning, assessment, and maintenance. Using advanced equipment and techniques, we capture critical structural data, alignment, and elevation details. Our surveys support safe and efficient bridge design, retrofitting, and infrastructure development across varied terrains and project scales.

Road Survey using DGPS and Total Station


We provide precise road survey solutions using advanced instruments like Total Station and DGPS. Our surveys support efficient planning, alignment, and construction of road infrastructure by providing accurate topographic and alignment data, ensuring smooth execution from design to completion with a focus on quality and compliance.

Layout Work


CAD Services delivers inch-perfect layout work for construction and infrastructure projects, ensuring accurate placement of structural elements as per design specifications. Using advanced surveying tools, our team translates plans into real-world coordinates, providing reliable groundwork for foundations, alignments, and installations to ensure smooth execution and structural integrity.

Center Line Marking


We offer faultless center line marking for roads, bridges, and construction layouts. Using advanced surveying tools, we ensure accurate alignment that supports structural integrity and smooth execution. Our experienced team delivers reliable results that meet engineering standards and contribute to efficient, error-free project development.

Contour Mapping


CAD Services delivers precise contour mapping to represent land elevation and terrain features accurately. Using advanced Total Station and DGPS technology, we provide detailed contour maps essential for planning, drainage, and construction projects. Our data-driven approach ensures reliable topographic insights for efficient and informed site development.

Training Program


At CAD Services, we offer practical, industry-oriented training programs in land surveying using advanced equipment like Total Station and DGPS. Designed for students, professionals, and technicians, our training combines theoretical knowledge with real-world fieldwork, ensuring participants gain hands-on experience and confidence to excel in surveying and infrastructure projects.


At CAD Services, we combine advanced technologies with industry experience to deliver results that meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency. Whether it's topographic surveys, land development planning, contour mapping, or CAD drafting, our team is dedicated to turning complex data into clear, actionable insights.
